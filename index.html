The Difference

Streaming convenience
vs. configured excellence.

Convenience platforms are designed for the average viewer. Cinema Machina is designed for you.

Streaming / Default Setup

  • Compressed video — 15–25 Mbps typical
  • Heavily processed audio — lossy Dolby Digital
  • HDR tone mapping decided by the platform
  • No lossless audio passthrough
  • Region-restricted content
  • Dependent on internet bandwidth
  • No director's cut versions
  • Interface not optimised for cinema viewing

Configured Playback System

  • Full bitrate remux — 50–120 Mbps
  • Lossless audio — Dolby TrueHD, DTS-HD MA, Atmos
  • HDR passthrough with proper tone mapping
  • Bit-perfect to your processor and display
  • Full library ownership — no dropouts
  • Zero buffering — local or fast LAN playback
  • Full editions — theatrical, extended, 4K remasters
  • Cinema-mode UI with frame rate matching
